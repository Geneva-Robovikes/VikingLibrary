Nidec Brushless Motor

What is it?

The Nidec Brushless motor is a brushless motor with a built in speed controller. Unlike speed controllers for brushed motors, the Nidec needs both a PWM port and a DIO port for control.

Properties

Output Port (PWM)
The PWM port on the roboRIO or MXP that the output is plugged into.
Output Port (DIO)
The DIO port on the roboRIO or MXP that the output is plugged into.
Inverted
Whether to invert the direction of the speed controller.
